Quick Notes
Data is based on Monday through Friday and excludes any UC Davis recognized holidays
Data inlcudes both salaried and hourly workers
Metrics Explained
Total Absent People = the total number of absent people during the month
Total Absent Hours = the total sum of absent hours for the month
Average Absenteeism Per Day (People) = total number of absent people for the month / total workdays in the month
Average Absenteeism Per Day (Hours) = the total sum of absent hours for the month / total workdays in the month
Total Hours Worked = the total sum of worked hours for the month, includes salary and non-salary employees
Absent Percentage = Total Absent Hours / Total Hours Worked
