Metrics Explained
Total Absent People = total amount of absent people from the chosen department for the month
Total Absent Hours = total amount of absent hours from the chosen department for the month
Average Absenteeism Per Day (People) = the average amount of absent people each day of the month
Average Absenteeism Per Day (Hours) = the average amount of absent hours absent each day of the month
Daily Absences Graph = the number of absences for each Monday – Friday day in the month
Average Absence By Day = the average amount of absences for each day of the week, Monday – Friday
Average Absence By Week = the average amount of absences for each day for the four weeks in the month
Example: 2021-08-02 = 1.2 would mean that the average absenteeism for any day in the week starting 2021-08-02 was 1.2 people.
Percent of Absence by Type = The percentage of absences that fall into each of the 5 categories: Vacation, Sick, Leave, FMLA, Emergency
Absent Percentage = Total Hours Absent/Total Hours Worked
Most Absent Days = Days of the month with the most absences
Type of Absence by Day of Week = The lighter the color the more absences, can also hover over the cell to get the exact number of absences.
